1
CMU wall with smooth stucco along the bottom and right perimeter — no columns. This is the boundary that defines the whole front yard feel. Needs to read clean and modern from the street.
2
Front entryway gate — dark grey decorative gate with a warm wood panel backdrop. The reference image shows the vibe exactly: stucco wall, warm wood door/panel, simple and architectural. This is the first impression.
3
Wood panel feature — 4'×1'×6' horizontal slat panel as a design element and privacy screen in the left zone. Reference image shows horizontal slats with greenery behind — that's exactly the look.
4
Concrete patio zone — 18'×8' on the left, with a concrete path connecting down to the front entryway. Simple, durable, ties into the existing hardscape language.
5
Garden bed with CMU raised planter — 13'6" wide along the house front, 3' deep. Softens the house edge. Ebru to confirm planting palette.
6
Master bed deck — 17'×14' in the top-right zone. This is Phase II / separate quote. Not blocking anything now but keeping it in the plan so Landscape Team designs around it.

Open questions at the bottom. Brian — please review and flag anything that doesn't work with the Phase I proposal scope. Ebru — your input on planting and gate color is needed before we can sign.
